Congratulations!  You've just taken an important step toward simplifying machine identity management and improving security for your organization.  

CyberArk *Workload Identity Manager* is a lightweight, easy to deploy containerized solution that goes anywhere just-in-time certificates are needed.  Unlike other PKI solutions that require heavy infrastructure or can't support the performance demands of modern architectures, *Workload Identity Manager* is ephemeral and generates keys and issues certificates at unprecedented speeds even under extreme load.

CyberArk *Workload Identity Manager* can also solve the problem of distributing enterprise trust with the help of  [Certificate Manager - SaaS](https://docs.venafi.cloud/firefly/overview/).  In this mode, the *Workload Identity Manager* CA certificate is issued by the enterprise PKI allowing application trust domains to extend beyond their borders.  Information Security teams also get to be responsible for the policies that govern certificates and keys while allowing Platform Engineers and Developers to be responsible for the availability of the certificate services on which their applications depend.
